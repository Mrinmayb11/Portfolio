PROCESS

Colors and font

I start things by choosing the right color and fonts.I get to know the brand inside out. like its objective, understanding the brand's personality and target audience. so the color and fonts that i choose reflect with the brand

Wireframe

Now thats all done. now its time to create the skeleton of the website. in this period, I lay the foundation for the website's structure and layout, making sure text, images and buttons are in the right places. no fancy visuals here, because it helps me to visualize the site's hierarchy and content placement without getting distracted by aesthetics.

Design

The magic happens here. now With the wireframe as my guide. i design the website. I make sure every little detail, from the spacing between elements to the choice of images and icons, is just right

Develoment

Time to turn those pretty designs into a living, breathing website.Depending on the project's requirements, I choose between two approaches: Webflow or coding with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript from scratch. Webflow helps me the flexibility to build responsive websites quickly, ensuring a smooth and efficient development process. On the other hand, custom coding helps me to fine-tune every aspect of the website. Whichever path I take, I make sure the website is smooth, optimized and fast.
